The communal sports and recreational facility

The communal sports and recreational facility communicates with residents mainly through posters (it has showcases located throughout the city and on social media). Due to the variety of classes, it was difficult for me to achieve graphic uniformity. The only element that binds the individual posters together is the gray beams at the top and bottom of the poster as well as subdued, pastel colors. Although the object lacks total graphic consistency, I am very happy with the posters. It also translated into commercial success (obviously I don't think that was the main factor) - The facility had to enroll online for the classes as too many people attended and they didn't fit the classrooms.
