Utility graphics

Recently, my city has undergone a revitalization of the city's information carriers. I had the pleasure to design the layout that is currently valid on all city boards. The combination of lime and blue colors looks great in print and refers to the coat of arms of Trzebnica, and the use of icons in the upper right corner allows you to identify at a glance what kind of plaque we are dealing with.

public transport

The case with the public transport plaque was very complicated. While line A is simple - the bus runs on a circular route all the time, line B was one big chaos. The B line bus never makes the same loop. He goes to different places all day long. Hence, my timetable is based on the timeline. Interestingly, it was the only project I did focus on research while working on. I wasn't sure about it, but it turned out to be a success. The project was my final thesis during my studies in UX classes.
