My name is Jakub Łączniak and I am a graphic designer with an ambition to be a front-end developer. I have been working as a freelancer for several years, and for over two years I have been a communication design specialist in my hometown of Trzebnica. In my daily work, I encounter a huge amount of very diverse tasks. Commercial graphics, preparing materials for printing, for the Internet, animation, film processing, photo retouching, illustration, branding of buildings, billboards, leaflets design, product photography, reporting, and from time to time even the work of a lecturer or film statistician. In general, little can surprise me, and I like this variety and I am not afraid of challenges.
